# Finance-Scenario Assumptions (Source-Grounded)

Source: `../../../../DGE-METRIC-VietNam/docs/financing_pathway.md`. Do not alter these figures without SME/user confirmation.

## Baseline facts

| Indicator | Value | Policy interpretation |
|---|---:|---|
| Emissions pathways | PDP8 and Net Zero (NZ) | Two policy environments for financing assessment |
| Public investment financing rate (baseline) | 8% | Reference cost of government capital |
| Concessional financing shock | −3 percentage points (effective 5%) | Reduced public financing cost |
| Revenue recycling baseline | None | Emission-tax revenue not earmarked for renewable investment |
| Revenue-recycling variant | Cap-and-trade revenue fully redirected to renewable capital subsidy (τ^{K,F}_Renewable,t = Rev^{C&T}_t) | Investment crowd-in channel |

## Six-scenario matrix

| Scenario ID | Emissions pathway | Public financing rate | Revenue recycling | Role |
|---|---|---:|---|---|
| PDP8-Base | PDP8 | 8% | No | Baseline under planned pathway |
| NZ-Base | Net Zero | 8% | No | Baseline under decarbonization pathway |

## Comparison rule

Compare concessional/recycling scenarios only against the base case with the **same** emissions pathway. Cross-pathway comparison (PDP8 vs. NZ) is a separate question about how financing tools interact with climate-policy stringency, not a substitute for the within-pathway comparison.
